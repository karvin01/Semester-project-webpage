The

Brothers

Lionheart

Join Skarntyden for a journey into the world of Astrid Lindgren in The Brothers Lionheart, which follows Jonathan and his younger brother Karl on an unexpected adventure from their home to Nangijala, the land where sagas are born. In this dreamlike realm, beauty and wonder abound, yet danger lies in the mountains beyond the valleys, where the evil Tengil and his dragon Katla threaten the peace.

Brought to life by our dedicated local ensemble at Musikhuset Esbjerg, this production highlights the powerful bond between the brothers as they confront difficult choices, loyalty, and fear. It is a timeless story that reminds us that courage often appears in unexpected moments and that there are things we must do to remain human, even when we are afraid.
