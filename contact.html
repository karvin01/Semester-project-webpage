About Us

We are a diverse community of creators aged 12 to 81, united by a passion for the stage. As the oldest amateur theater group in Esbjerg, our mission is to introduce as many people as possible to the magic of theater.

From our large-scale winter plays at Musikhuset to our free outdoor summer comedies. Whether on stage or behind the scenes, we are united by our love for the craft.

Where Does Your Donation Go?

Help Us Keep the Spotlight On!

Skarntyden is run entirely by volunteers, from our 12-year-old debut actors to our 81-year-old veterans. We do this because we love telling stories and bringing Esbjerg together.

However, theater requires more than just passion. Your donation ensures we can continue to offer workshops, rent professional venues, and keep the oldest amateur theater in Esbjerg alive for the next generation. Every kroner helps us create "visions of pure enchantment" without breaking the bank.
